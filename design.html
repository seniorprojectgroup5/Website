The Queen Bee The Design Process The team

The Neckband

The neckband of the Queen Bee is designed to rest around the user's neck. Our design will allow it to be flexed and positioned for a customizable fit and placement of the arms of the neckband and, thus, the haptic motors providing vibration. The back of the neckband will contain the remaining hardware in a "bubble" type shape that will stick out from the back of the neck and elongate down, such that the neckband can remain discrete. For final production, the neckband will be airbrush painted and branded.

The Application

The pieces of our mobile application — functionality and design — will be built in tandem with each other. Functionality will begin programming immediately to get proofs of concept complete and working as they should for easier implementation once the design is ready. Meanwhile, the design will undergo testing to ensure it is clear, usable, and well-designed. As the design pieces get solidified through user testing, they will be programmed and have functionality applied to the elements of the design to create a fully functional and designed application.
